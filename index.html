about me

Since I was young I've been passionate for illustration and design and during high school I started getting more into it again. When I started my studies in Visual design at LUCA School of Arts Ghent I discovered my passion for more simplistic illustration and also animation.

In animation I specialize in 2D After Effects animation and I recently started getting more into frame-by-frame animation as well. Over the years I've had a wide variety of different illustration styles but lately I am obsessed by vector illustration

Besides visuals I started playing piano and producing music since I was 11 years old and I am still in love with doing it to this day.
