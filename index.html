about me

Ever since I was young, I have had a passion for illustration and design. My interest and skills blossomed throughout high school, leading me to study Visual Design at LUCA School of Arts Ghent. Through this course, I refined my skills, technique and style and discovered my passion for simplistic, stylised illustration and animation.

I specialise in 2D After Effects animation and have been focussed on this for a few years, both personally and commercially. I am also gaining experience and broadening my skills with 3D animation, both through my studies and personal projects. Over the years I have explored a wide variety of different illustration and animation styles but specialise in vector illustration. If you have a specific style you want brought to life, or just a general idea, please get in touch!

I have experience working with clients commercially in many areas, and many examples of this can be seen in my portfolio above or on my social media pages. Send me an email or an instagram message if you have any other questions about my work, prices, availability or a specific project enquiry!
